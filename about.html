about us

OUR MISSION

We see ourselves to be nothing but the best in the global food industry through perpetual innovation in our processes, application of state-of-the-art technology, product creation, human resource development and in turn delighted consumers.

OUR VISION

Our goal is to expand the capacity and sales of Winner Foods by five-folds before 2020 through introducing a wide bouquet of novel products in the market and establishing a substantial identity in the food industry of Pakistan. We aim to provide our customers with the best quality product that money can buy with easy accessibility. We are not only penetrating all metros, villages and towns of Pakistan, but also plan to enter at least 10 foreign countries through creating multiple alliances and partners. Our focus will also be towards keeping stakeholders content with our progress and conducting all operations in a manner where safety and health of our workers is secured, along with the preservation of our environment.

OUR COMPANY

Winner Foods is an emerging and rapidly growing concern in the food industry of Pakistan with a vast portfolio of products in the market and under development. It established a name for itself in corn based pops market by introduction of Jungle Pops in the year 2000 and capturing a major chunk of the market. Ever since, the company has dedicated its efforts towards crafting new products instead of following the competitive leaders of the industry. Today, Winner Food continues to grow at an exponential rate with its strategy of creating and selling one of its kind products in the market. It aims to go beyond borders of Pakistan and introducing their range to several untapped international markets in the near future.
